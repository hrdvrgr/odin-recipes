Thin beef curry

Description

Swedish-style curry beef slices served with rice

Ingredients

  • 800g thinly sliced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 200 ml Norrmejerier whipping cream
  • approx. 100 ml water
  • 2 bouillon cube
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sambal oelek

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into slices, same with onion (Async start cooking rice)
  2. Fry the meat for a bit then add onion slices
  3. Put everything else in
  4. Let it boil until ur happy
  5. Serve with rice
