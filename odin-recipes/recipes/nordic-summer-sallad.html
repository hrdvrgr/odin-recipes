Nordic summer sallad

Description

Nordic summer salad with cheese and wallnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh arugula
  • 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped walnuts
  • 200 ml Norrmejerier whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix arugula, strawberries, feta, and walnuts.
  2. In a small jar, whisk honey, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad and gently toss.
  4. Serve chilled with a slice of crusty sourdough bread.
